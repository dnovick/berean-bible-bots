# Parsing Drill

*Chapter 13 — Qal Perfect Strong Verbs*

Identify the person, number, gender, and root (3ms lexical form) for each of 25 Qal Perfect forms.

## Coverage

| Part | Description | Items |
|---|---|---|
| Part A | Clear suffix pattern; all strong roots | 10 |
| Part B | Vowel reduction in R1 (3cp, 2mp, 2fp) and accent variation | 10 |
| Part C | Stative roots — identify type (A/B/C) and give the 3ms dictionary form | 5 |

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 שָׁמַרְתָּ 1 2 3 s p du m f c
> **A:** ✓ שָׁמַרְתָּ 2 s m שמר

**Q2.** 2 כָּתַבְתִּי 1 2 3 s p du m f c
> **A:** ✓ כָּתַבְתִּי 1 s c כתב

**Q3.** 3 שָׁמְרוּ 1 2 3 s p du m f c
> **A:** ✓ שָׁמְרוּ 3 p c שמר
