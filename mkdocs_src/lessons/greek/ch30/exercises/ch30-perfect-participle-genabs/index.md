# Perfect Participle Genabs

*Chapter 30 — Combinative (Perfect) Participles and Genitive Absolutes*

A 15-item drill: items 1–8 parse perfect participle forms (active and middle/passive); items 9–15 identify and translate genitive absolute constructions. Students identify reduplication, parse fully, and translate. An answer key is embedded in all formats.

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 λελυκώς Perf καθημένου λέγοντος λαλοῦντος τεθνηκότος ἀπελθόντος ἐκπορευομένου ὄντος Active Aor Mid/Pass Perf Pres Acc Act Dat Mid Nom Gen Pl Sg Fem Masc Sg &#9654;
> **A:** 1 λελυκώς Perf Active Nom Sg Masc λύω The one who has loosed the slaves departed.

**Q2.** 2 πεπιστευκότας Perf καθημένου λέγοντος λαλοῦντος τεθνηκότος ἀπελθόντος ἐκπορευομένου ὄντος Active Aor Mid/Pass Perf Pres Acc Act Dat Mid Nom Gen Pl Sg Fem Masc Sg &#9654;
> **A:** 2 πεπιστευκότας Perf Active Acc Pl Masc πιστεύω He saw those who had believed in God.

**Q3.** 3 γεγραφυῖα Perf καθημένου λέγοντος λαλοῦντος τεθνηκότος ἀπελθόντος ἐκπορευομένου ὄντος Active Aor Mid/Pass Perf Pres Acc Act Dat Mid Nom Gen Pl Sg Fem Masc Sg &#9654;
> **A:** 3 γεγραφυῖα Perf Active Nom Sg Fem γράφω The woman who had written in the book.
