# Passage Exercise

*Chapter 16 — Qal Imperfect Weak Verbs*

Parse weak-root Qal Imperfect and Wayyiqtol forms drawn from Genesis.

## Coverage

| Part | Description | Items |
|---|---|---|
| Passage A | III-ה and III-א forms from Genesis 1–3 | 5 |
| Passage B | III-guttural and I-guttural forms from Genesis 3–4 | 5 |
| Passage C | I-נ and I-י forms from Genesis 4–9 | 5 |
| Passage D | Biconsonantal and Geminate forms from Genesis 19–22 | 5 |

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 יְהִ֣י
> **A:** ✓ יְהִ֣י 3 s m הָיָה III-ה Jussive Volitional — short jussive: final ה dropped; "Let there be light" — divine fiat

**Q2.** 2 יַעֲשֶׂ֥ה
> **A:** ✓ יַעֲשֶׂ֥ה 3 s m עָשָׂה III-ה + I-gutt. Jussive Volitional — III-ה long jussive (seghol-he); I-gutt. ע causes chateph prefix; "Let the earth produce"

**Q3.** 3 יִהְי֣וּ
> **A:** ✓ יִהְי֣וּ 3 p m הָיָה III-ה Imperfect Jussive/Volitional — 3mp of הָיָה; וּ- suffix replaces ה; "Let there be lights"
