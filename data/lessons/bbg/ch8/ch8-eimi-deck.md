## How to Use

- **Front of card:** inflected Greek form
- **Back of card:** verb + tense/mood + person + number + gloss
- **Tags** allow filtering by person or number in Anki

> **Key features of εἰμί:**
> - εἰμί is a **linking verb** (copula) — it connects a subject to a predicate nominative or predicate adjective, not to a direct object.
> - It is **enclitic** in all forms except the 2nd singular (εἶ) and the infinitive. Enclitics attach their accent to the preceding word; do not expect εἰμί forms to carry their own accent in most contexts.
> - The **moveable nu** appears on ἐστίν before a vowel and at the end of a clause; before a consonant the form is ἐστί.
> - εἰμί has **no voice** (it is neither active nor passive) and **no aorist or perfect** in NT Greek.
