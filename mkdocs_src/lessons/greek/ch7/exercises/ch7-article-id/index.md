# Article Id

*Chapter 7 — Genitive and Dative*

[Full Screen (Interactive)](ch7-article-id.html){.md-button .md-button--primary}  [Print / PDF](ch7-article-id.pdf){.md-button}

20-item exercise drawn from GNT passages. Each item shows an article form in its verse context. Students identify the case, number, and gender of the article.

Covers all five cases (Nom, Gen, Dat, Acc, Nom./Acc.) and all three genders (Masculine, Feminine, Neuter) across singular and plural forms.

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 ὁ Acc. Dat. Gen. Nom. Nom./Acc. Pl. Sg. Fem. Masc. Masc./Neut. Neut. &#9654;
> **A:** 1 ὁ Jhn 1:1 Nom. Sg. Masc.

**Q2.** 2 τοῦ Acc. Dat. Gen. Nom. Nom./Acc. Pl. Sg. Fem. Masc. Masc./Neut. Neut. &#9654;
> **A:** 2 τοῦ Jhn 5:43 Gen. Sg. Masc.

**Q3.** 3 τὸν Acc. Dat. Gen. Nom. Nom./Acc. Pl. Sg. Fem. Masc. Masc./Neut. Neut. &#9654;
> **A:** 3 τὸν Jhn 3:16 Acc. Sg. Masc.
