# Adjective Number Drill

*Chapter 10 — Adjectives and Numbers*

A 20-item drill covering adjectives and numbers from BBA Chapter 10 (*Basics of Biblical Aramaic*, Van Pelt). Each item presents an Aramaic word or phrase; the student identifies whether it is an adjective or number, gives the state/value and gender/number, and translates.

## Coverage

| Types Covered |
|---|
| Attributive adjectives (absolute, determined) |
| Ordinal adjectives (first, second, third) |
| Cardinal numbers 1–8 with nouns |
| Indefinite article use of חַד |
| Number gender polarity with nouns |
