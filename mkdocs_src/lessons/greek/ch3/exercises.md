| Exercise | Description |
|---|---|
| [Alphabet Drill](exercises/ch3-alphabet-drill/index.md) | A 24-item drill covering all letters of the Greek alphabet. Students identify the letter name and its pronunciation/sound from the written lowercase (and occasional uppercase) form. The drill reinforces both letter recognition and the phonetic value of each letter, including final sigma and the gamma nasal. |
