# Present Active Parsing

*Chapter 16 — Present Active Indicative*

## Coverage

| Feature | Forms drilled |
|---|---|
| Person | 1st, 2nd, 3rd |
| Number | Singular and plural |
| Verbs | Regular thematic verbs (no contract verbs — see Ch17) |

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 λύω
> **A:** Pres. Act. Ind. · 1st · Singular · λύω — "I am loosing / I loose"

**Q2.** 2 λύεις
> **A:** Pres. Act. Ind. · 2nd · Singular · λύω — "You are loosing"

**Q3.** 3 λύει
> **A:** Pres. Act. Ind. · 3rd · Singular · λύω — "He/she/it is loosing"
