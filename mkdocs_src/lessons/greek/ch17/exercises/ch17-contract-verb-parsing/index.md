# Contract Verb Parsing

*Chapter 17 — Contract Verbs*

## Coverage

| Contract class | Representative verb | Forms drilled |
|---|---|---|
| α-contract (-αω) | ἀγαπάω | All 6 persons |
| ε-contract (-εω) | ποιέω, λαλέω, ζητέω | All 6 persons |
| ο-contract (-οω) | πληρόω | Selected forms |

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 ἀγαπῶ α
> **A:** 1st · Sg · ἀγαπάω | α + ω → ω | "I love"

**Q2.** 2 ἀγαπᾷς α
> **A:** 2nd · Sg · ἀγαπάω | α + ει → ᾳ (subscript iota) | "You love"

**Q3.** 3 ἀγαπᾷ α
> **A:** 3rd · Sg · ἀγαπάω | α + ει → ᾷ | "He/she loves"
