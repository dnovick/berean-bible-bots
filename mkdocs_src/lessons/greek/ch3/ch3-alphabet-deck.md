## How to Use

- **Front of card:** lowercase form (uppercase in parentheses)
- **Back of card:** letter name + transliteration + pronunciation hint
- Sigma's two lowercase forms (σ / ς) are on a single card
- **Tags** allow filtering by letter type (vowel, labial, velar, dental, liquid, nasal, sibilant, double)
