## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 גֻּלָּה גלה III-ה Perfect 3ms
> **A:** Piel active: to reveal / uncover &nbsp;|&nbsp; Pual passive: it was revealed &nbsp;·&nbsp; Qibbuts under ג + dagesh in ל + final ָּה (III-ה pattern)

**Q2.** 2 כֻּלָּה כלה III-ה Perfect 3ms
> **A:** Piel active: to complete / destroy &nbsp;|&nbsp; Pual passive: it was finished / consumed &nbsp;·&nbsp; Qibbuts under כ + dagesh in ל + final ָּה

**Q3.** 3 בֹּרַךְ ברך R2 = ר Perfect 3ms
> **A:** Piel active: to bless &nbsp;|&nbsp; Pual passive: he was blessed &nbsp;·&nbsp; Holem under ב (qibbuts lengthened; ר rejects dagesh) + patach under ר
