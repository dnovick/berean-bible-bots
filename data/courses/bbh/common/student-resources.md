## Bible Study Software

You need a tool that supports:

- Full morphological tagging of the Hebrew OT (BHS / Westminster Morphology or equivalent)
- **Lemma search** — find all occurrences of a root across the OT
- **Morphology search** — filter by stem, conjugation, PGN (e.g., all Qal perfect 3ms verbs)
- **Interlinear or word-level gloss display** — hover or inline English glosses
- Verse display with vowel pointing and cantillation marks

### Paid Options (Recommended if affordable)

| Tool | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **[Logos Bible Software](https://www.logos.com)** | Best-in-class morphology search; integrates with the BBH textbook resource; supports clause-level searches; cross-platform (Mac/Win/iOS/Android). Starter packages begin ~$50; full Hebrew capability requires at least the **Foundations** package or equivalent. |
| **[Accordance](https://www.accordancebible.com)** | Mac-native; excellent morphology engine; slightly steeper learning curve for beginners but very powerful. Hebrew OT module required (~$70+). |

**Class recommendation: Logos** — the textbook is available as a Logos resource, search syntax is well-documented, and free training videos are available.

### Free Options

| Tool | Morphology Search | Lemma Search | Interlinear | Notes |
|------|:-----------------:|:------------:|:-----------:|-------|
| **[STEP Bible](https://www.stepbible.org)** | Yes | Yes | Yes | Full OSHB morphology; boolean morph search; free; web-based; actively maintained by Tyndale House. **Class recommendation.** |
| **[Logos Web App](https://www.logos.com)** | Limited | Yes | Yes | Free account gives access to base texts and some morphology. |
| **[Biblia.com](https://biblia.com)** | Limited | Yes | Yes | Logos-lite web interface; no advanced morphology filter. |

| **[Sefaria](https://www.sefaria.org)** | No | Partial | Partial | Strong on rabbinic texts; Hebrew OT present but morphology search limited. |
