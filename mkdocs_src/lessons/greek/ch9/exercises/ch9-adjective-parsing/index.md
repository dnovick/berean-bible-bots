A 20-item parsing drill covering 2-1-2 Greek adjectives in context. Students parse each adjective phrase for case, number, gender, and then identify whether the adjective is in attributive position, predicate position, or substantival use. Items are drawn from common GNT adjectives and vocabulary introduced through Chapter 9.

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 ὁ ἀγαθὸς δοῦλος &#9658; Answer
> **A:** N · S · M · Attributive — art-adj-noun; "the good slave"

**Q2.** 2 ὁ δοῦλος ἀγαθός &#9658; Answer
> **A:** N · S · M · Predicate — noun has art; adj has none; "The slave is good."

**Q3.** 3 οἱ ἀγαθοί &#9658; Answer
> **A:** N · P · M · Substantival — art + adj; no noun; "the good people"
