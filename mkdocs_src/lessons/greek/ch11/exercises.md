# Ch11 — First and Second Person Personal Pronouns: Exercises

[← Back to lesson](index.md)

| Exercise | Description |
|---|---|
| [Pronoun Parsing](exercises/ch11-pronoun-parsing/index.md) | A 20-item parsing drill covering 1st and 2nd person pronoun forms. Students identify the person (1st/2nd), case (Nom/Gen/Dat/Acc), number (Sg/Pl), and provide an English translation of the form. Items include both emphatic (accented) and unemphatic (enclitic) forms, and cover all four cases in both singular and plural for each person. |
