A 20-item parsing drill on ἵστημι, τίθημι, δείκνυμι, ἀφίημι, ἀπόλλυμι, and οἶδα. Students parse each form (tense / voice / person / number / mood / lexical form) and translate. Items include the transitive/intransitive ἵστημι distinction, perfect-with-present-meaning (ἕστηκα, οἶδα), and common GNT contexts. An answer key is embedded in all formats.

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 ἔστησεν αὐτὸ ἐν μέσῳ αὐτῶν.
> **A:** Aor (1st) · Act · 3rd · Sg · ἵστημι · Transitive · "He set it in their midst." (Matt 18:2)

**Q2.** 2 ἔστη Ἰησοῦς εἰς τὸ μέσον καὶ λέγει αὐτοῖς· εἰρήνη ὑμῖν.
> **A:** Aor (2nd) · Act · 3rd · Sg · ἵστημι · Intransitive · "Jesus stood in their midst and says: Peace to you." (John 20:19)

**Q3.** 3 ἕστηκεν ἐν τῷ ἁγίῳ τόπῳ τὸ βδέλυγμα τῆς ἐρημώσεως.
> **A:** Perf · Act · 3rd · Sg · ἵστημι · Intransitive (= is standing) · "The abomination of desolation is standing in the holy place." (Matt 24:15)
