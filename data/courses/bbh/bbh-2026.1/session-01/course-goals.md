# Course Goals

## Course Outline
- Biblical Hebrew Alphabet - Consonants and Vowels
- Biblical Hebrew Morphology
  - Biblical Hebrew Nouns
  - Biblical Hebrew Verbs

## Course Non-Goals
- Mishnaic, Later Religious, and Modern Hebrew
- Biblical Greek
- Biblical Aramaic
- In-Depth Phonology and Orthography
- Hebrew Syntax
- Hebrew Accents
- Hebrew Genre Studies (e.g., Poetry)
- Discourse Analysis
