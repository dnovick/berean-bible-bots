## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 יֻּגַּד Wayyiqtol 3ms Gen 22:20 "it was told to Abraham" PT LF SR SC SA
> **A:** SR — Speech Reporting · Root: נָגַד · Information was reported to Abraham; Hophal of Hiphil "to tell/report"

**Q2.** 2 יּוּשַׂם Wayyiqtol 3ms Gen 24:33 "food was set before him" PT LF SR SC SA
> **A:** SC — State Change · Root: שִׂים · Food was caused to be placed/set — entered the state of being present before him

**Q3.** 3 הוּרַד Qatal 3ms Gen 39:1 "he had been brought down to Egypt" PT LF SR SC SA
> **A:** PT — Physical Transfer · Root: יָרַד · Joseph was physically taken down to Egypt; Hiphil = to bring down
