# Chapter 7 — Definite Article Paradigm Deck

*17 cards covering the complete definite article paradigm across all three genders.*
*Import `ch7-article-deck.txt` directly into Anki (File → Import).*

---

## How to Use

- **Front of card:** Greek article form
- **Back of card:** part of speech + gender(s) + case + number + gloss
- Forms shared across genders list all valid parsings on the back
- **Tags** allow filtering by gender or case in Anki

> **Note:** The definite article has no vocative. The neuter τό serves as both nom sg and acc sg; τά as both nom pl and acc pl. The masculine and neuter share all genitive and dative forms (τοῦ, τῷ, τῶν, τοῖς) — context and the noun's ending distinguish gender.
