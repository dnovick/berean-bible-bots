| Exercise | Description |
|---|---|
| [Third Decl Parsing](exercises/ch10-third-decl-parsing/index.md) | A 20-item parsing drill covering 3rd-declension noun forms drawn from common GNT vocabulary. Students identify the case, number, gender, and lexical form for each form. Items include consonant-stem nouns (velar, dental, liquid), neuter -μα nouns, and i-stem nouns. The definite article is included on most items to aid in case identification; some items are anarthrous to increase challenge. |
