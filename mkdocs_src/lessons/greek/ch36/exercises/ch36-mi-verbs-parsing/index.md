*Chapter 36 — ἵστημι, τίθημι, δείκνυμι and Odds 'n Ends*

A 20-item parsing drill on ἵστημι, τίθημι, δείκνυμι, ἀφίημι, ἀπόλλυμι, and οἶδα. Students parse each form (tense / voice / person / number / mood / lexical form) and translate. Items include the transitive/intransitive ἵστημι distinction, perfect-with-present-meaning (ἕστηκα, οἶδα), and common GNT contexts. An answer key is embedded in all formats.

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 ἔστησεν Aor Aor 1st Aor 2nd Fut Perf Pres Act Mid 1st 2nd 3rd Acc Pl Sg Sg Neut Imper Ind Inf Ptc I T &#9654;
> **A:** 1 ἔστησεν Aor 1st Act 3rd Sg Ind ἵστημι T He set a child in their midst.

**Q2.** 2 ἔστη Aor Aor 1st Aor 2nd Fut Perf Pres Act Mid 1st 2nd 3rd Acc Pl Sg Sg Neut Imper Ind Inf Ptc I T &#9654;
> **A:** 2 ἔστη Aor 2nd Act 3rd Sg Ind ἵστημι I Jesus stood in their midst and said: Peace to you.

**Q3.** 3 ἕστηκεν Aor Aor 1st Aor 2nd Fut Perf Pres Act Mid 1st 2nd 3rd Acc Pl Sg Sg Neut Imper Ind Inf Ptc I T &#9654;
> **A:** 3 ἕστηκεν Perf Act 3rd Sg Ind ἵστημι I The abomination of desolation is standing in the holy place.
