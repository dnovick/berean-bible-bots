# Passage Exercise

*Chapter 15 — Qal Imperfect Strong Verbs*

Parse 15 Qal Imperfect verbs from Exodus 3–4 (the burning bush narrative) and Exodus 20
(the Decalogue). All target forms emphasize the prefix pattern; weak roots are noted.

## Coverage

| Usage Type | Count |
|---|---|
| Simple Future | 6 |
| Jussive | 3 |
| Prohibition (לֹא/אַל + Imperfect) | 4 |
| Habitual/Repeated | 2 |

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 אֵלֵ֖ךְ
> **A:** ✓ אֵלֵ֖ךְ 1 s c הלך Cohortative — "Let me go"; I-י weak with special he drop; volitional 1cs

**Q2.** 2 אֶרְאֶ֗ה
> **A:** ✓ אֶרְאֶ֗ה 1 s c ראה Cohortative — III-ה weak; "Let me see" — parallel volitional

**Q3.** 3 תִקְרַב
> **A:** ✓ תִקְרַב 2 s m קרב Prohibition (לֹא) — B-class patach under R2; "do not come near"
