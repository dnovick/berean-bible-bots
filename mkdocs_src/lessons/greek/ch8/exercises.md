|---|---|
| [Preposition Parsing](exercises/ch8-preposition-parsing/index.md) | A 20-item drill drawn from GNT prepositional phrases. Students identify the preposition, the case it governs in each instance, and produce a contextual English translation of the phrase. Items cover the major prepositions from Chapter 8 including multi-case prepositions (διά, κατά, μετά, ἐπί, παρά, περί, ὑπό) where the case distinguishes the meaning. |
