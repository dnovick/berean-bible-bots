Identify the person, number, gender, and root for each of 25 Qal Imperfect forms.

## Coverage

| Part | Description | Items |
|---|---|---|
| Part A | Clear prefix pattern; A-class (holem) roots | 10 |
| Part B | B-class (patach) and stative roots; 2ms/3fs disambiguation | 10 |
| Part C | Jussives and cohortatives — identify form type and parse | 5 |

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 יִשְׁמֹר 1 2 3 s p du m f c
> **A:** ✓ יִשְׁמֹר 3 s m שמר

**Q2.** 2 תִּכְתְּבוּ 1 2 3 s p du m f c
> **A:** ✓ תִּכְתְּבוּ 2 p m כתב

**Q3.** 3 נִפְקֹד 1 2 3 s p du m f c
> **A:** ✓ נִפְקֹד 1 p c פקד
