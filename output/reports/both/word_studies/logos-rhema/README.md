# Word Study: λόγος vs ῥῆμα — "Word"

← [Back to Word Studies](../README.md)

A cross-corpus study comparing the two main Greek terms for "word" (G3056 / G4487) in the LXX and NT, including a sliding-window analysis of uses that refer to Scripture and an LLM-classified proximity table.
