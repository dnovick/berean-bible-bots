# Letter Recognition

*Chapter 1 — Hebrew Alphabet*

[Full Screen (Interactive)](ch1-letter-recognition.html){.md-button .md-button--primary}  [Print / PDF](ch1-letter-recognition.pdf){.md-button}  [Markdown](ch1-letter-recognition.md){.md-button}

33-item identification drill covering all 22 Hebrew consonants, the 5 final (sofit) forms, and all 6 begadkephat letters with dagesh lene, presented in mixed order. Students identify the letter name and sound for each item.

## Coverage

| Category | Count |
|---|---|
| Standard letters | 22 |
| Final (sofit) forms | 5 |
| Begadkephat with dagesh lene | 6 |
| **Total** | **33** |

## Sample Questions

**Q1.** 1 ק
> **A:** ✓ ק Qoph q (uvular); no English equivalent

**Q2.** 2 ם
> **A:** ✓ ם Mem Sofit m — final form of מ (occurs word-finally only)

**Q3.** 3 בּ
> **A:** ✓ בּ Beth (hard) b as in "boy" — dagesh lene; hard form
